Our Story

From a Small Team to a Global Practice

KoinWorx was founded in 2009 by a group of enterprise technology veterans who believed that mid-market companies deserved the same quality of technology services as the Fortune 500 — without the enterprise price tag.

What started as a software development shop in the UK quickly grew into a multi-disciplinary technology firm with offices in the United States, Pakistan, and the UAE. Today, our 200+ professionals serve clients across financial services, healthcare, retail, logistics, and more.

We remain independently owned, client-centric, and obsessively focused on delivery quality. Every engagement is treated as a long-term partnership — not a transaction.
