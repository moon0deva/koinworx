FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can you start a project? +

For staffing, we can typically have candidates in front of you within 2–3 business days and onboard within 10. For project work, we can usually begin within 2–4 weeks of contract signature, depending on scope and team requirements.

Do you work with companies outside your office countries? +

Absolutely. While our offices are in the US, UK, Pakistan, and UAE, we work with clients globally. Most of our engagement is delivered remotely, with on-site visits for critical milestones when needed.

What engagement sizes do you work with? +

We work with engagements from single-specialist staffing through to full-scale enterprise transformation programs. Our sweet spot is mid-market to enterprise clients, but we take selected startup projects where the technology is a good fit.

How does your pricing work? +

We offer fixed-price, time & materials, and managed service pricing models. Staffing is billed on a daily or monthly rate depending on engagement length. Contact us for a bespoke quote aligned to your specific requirements.
